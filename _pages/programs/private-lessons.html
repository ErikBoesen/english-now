Study at Your Own Pace

Our online private lessons provide one-on-one instruction of the highest quality. Each lesson is tailored to meet the specific need of the student whether it's conversation, pronunciation, writing, TOEFL preparation, interviewing techniques, presentation skills, or just about anything else!

Our flexible scheduling allows you to find a time and place that fits your lifestyle. Private lessons can be scheduled day or evening, Monday through Saturday, and we teach online on Zoom, making this convenient for you wherever you are.

We teach students of all levels. Students at higher levels of English proficiency may benefit from a curriculum that we often use called Professional English in Use. Published by Cambridge University Press, this curriculum is available for professionals in health sciences, business, law, and IT, and many high-level students feel that it provides them the tools that they need to use English more effectively.

Students at lower levels of English proficiency often use online curriculum, or sometimes one of our multi-level, general English curricula. Online private lesson students are invited to join our Friday and other outings, as we very much hope they can be part of the life of the school.

Our reasonable rates make private lessons a great value. Tuition for private lessons held online is $30 per hour.

There is no registration fee for private lessons. Books may cost extra, or your instructor may recommend online materials. Please let us know if you already have books and materials that you would like to use for your lessons.

In the event that you are not satisfied with your lessons for any reason, we are glad to refund unused tuition monies.
